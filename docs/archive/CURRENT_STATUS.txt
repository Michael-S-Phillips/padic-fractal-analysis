  Type: Hierarchical P-adic Analysis
  Complexity: O(n log n) for n pixels
  Foundation: P-adic ultrametric spaces
  Validation: Rigorously mathematical

PERFORMANCE:
  DEM Loading: <1 second
  Preprocessing: 1-5 seconds
  Pyramid Construction: 2-5 seconds
  Fractal Density: 10-30 seconds (O(n log n))
  GeoTIFF Export: 2-5 seconds

  DEM Storage: 4 MB (1K×1K float32)
  Pyramid (4/3x): 6 MB

SCALABILITY:

SUPPORTED DATA:
  Format: GeoTIFF (standard Mars DEM format)

  Coordinate System: Mars 2000 Equirectangular
  Data Type: Float32 elevation
